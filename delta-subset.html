The smallest \(\delta-\)sufficient subset \(S_\delta\) is the smallest subset of \(A_x\) satisfying \[P(x\in S_\delta) \geq 1 - \delta\]

Shannon's source coding theorem

let \(X\) be an ensemble with entropy \(H(X) = H\) bits. Given \(\epsilon \gt 0\) and \(0 \lt \delta \gt 1\), there exists a positive integer \(N_0\) such that for \(N \gt N_0\) $$|{1 \over N}H_\delta(X^N) - H| \lt \epsilon$$
